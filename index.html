BENEFITS OF MONOCRYSTALLINE PERC SOLar PANELS :-

Highly Efficient: Such types of solar panels are made using Monocrystalline PERC solar cells convert the maximum amount of solar energy into electricity. If you want to maximize electricity generation using solar energy from a specific area, such as your roof, then Monocrystalline Perc solar panels are the best choice. o PERC technology: These solar panels come with PERC (Passivated Emitter and Rear Cell) technology in which additional back layer film is etched behind the solar cells which helps in absorption of scattered or reflected sunlight resulting in higher power generation and achieving panel efficiency of up to 20%. o Space Efficient: Monocrystalline PERC solar panels can generate more power in less rooftop area and take less space for installation leading to reduction in Balance of System (BOS) costs and making them space efficient solar panels .
